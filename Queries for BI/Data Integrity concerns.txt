In this connection, we are taking all the billable calls from a specific time period. From dwh_ctmdb.calls, we have everything we need to determine the following details about all the billable calls we have for that time period:
1. Branch
2. Source (e.g. elocal or Google Ads, etc)
3. Month
4. Year 
5. The number of calls grouped by each above category

We are connecting this to dwh_reportsdb.subscription my means of a join on subscription and customer, where customer is joined with the above data through the primary phone number.
We are then adding an additional category of Service Type to the above count groups.

This second connection is more tenuous for the following reasons:
1. We don't know whether a call resulted in a subscription 
	(i.e. The call may be incorrectly ascribed to one subscription rather than another)
2. We don't know whether a subscription resulted from a call 
	(i.e. A subscription may be the result of outbound efforts rather than an inbound call)
3. We don't know whether a single call resulted in several subscriptions, thus leaving at least one service type null
